If you want to put some ❤️ into your website, you can go to the Unicode Party site to find hundreds of emojis that you can either directly copy and paste into the text of your website which will appear as emojis in your editor and on your website.

Emojis:

  • are easy to search and find
  • are easy to copy and paste
  • can give your website a playful touch
  • can be used as a simpler replacement for e.g. Font Awesome icons
  • can be used as bullet points as in this list
